# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to making participation in the Sentinex community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and constructive in discussions and reviews.
- Accepting feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and project users.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting comments, and deliberate disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.

## Reporting
Report unacceptable behavior via a private GitHub security advisory or by contacting the maintainers listed in `SECURITY.md`.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
